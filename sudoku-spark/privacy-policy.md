# Privacy Policy for Sudoku Spark™

**Last Updated: January 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Sudoku Spark™, developed by Khelnedo Games™. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information Collected Automatically

When you use Sudoku Spark, we may automatically collect:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App interactions, features used, session duration, game statistics
- **Crash Reports**: Technical information to help us fix bugs and improve performance
- **Advertising Data**: Ad interaction data for serving rewarded advertisements

### Information You Provide

- **Game Progress**: Puzzles completed, scores, achievements, and streaks (stored locally on your device)
- **Preferences**: Theme settings, game options, notification preferences

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide and maintain** the app's functionality
2. **Improve** our app based on usage patterns
3. **Fix bugs** and optimize performance
4. **Send notifications** (only if you've enabled them)
5. **Display advertisements** (rewarded ads for hints)

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
Your game progress, statistics, and preferences are stored **locally on your device**. This data is:
- Backed up to Google Drive (if you have Android backup enabled)
- Transferred to new devices during device migration
- Deleted when you uninstall the app (unless backed up)

### Cloud Services
We use cloud services for analytics and crash reporting. These services collect anonymous, aggregated data to help us improve the app.

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

### Google Firebase Analytics
- **Purpose**: Anonymous app analytics and crash reporting
- **Data collected**: Usage statistics, crash reports, device information
- **Privacy Policy**: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Google AdMob
- **Purpose**: Display rewarded advertisements
- **Data collected**: Advertising identifiers, ad interaction data
- **Privacy Policy**: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Google Play Services
- **Purpose**: In-app reviews, app updates
- **Privacy Policy**: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Advertising

Sudoku Spark displays **rewarded advertisements** that you can choose to watch in exchange for in-game hints. These ads are:
- **Optional**: You are never required to watch ads
- **Non-personalized**: We do not use your personal data for ad targeting
- **Provided by Google AdMob**: Subject to Google's privacy practices

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
- Collect or store personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, etc.)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Delete your data**: Uninstall the app to remove all local data
2. **Opt out of analytics**: Use your device's privacy settings to limit data collection
3. **Manage backups**: Control cloud backups through your Google account settings
4. **Disable notifications**: Turn off notifications in your device settings or within the app
5. **Reset advertising ID**: Reset your advertising identifier through device settings

## Children's Privacy

Sudoku Spark is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not:
- Require account creation
- Collect names, emails, or addresses
- Include social features or chat
- Display personalized advertisements

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
- All game data is stored locally on your device
- No sensitive personal information is collected or transmitted
- Analytics data is anonymized before transmission

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Displaying a notice in the app (for significant changes)

By using Sudoku Spark, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
